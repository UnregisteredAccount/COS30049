Learn About Air Quality

Air Quality Index (AQI) Levels

The Air Quality Index is a standardized way to measure and communicate air quality to the public. Understanding these levels helps you make informed decisions about outdoor activities.

Good (0-50)

Air quality is satisfactory and poses little or no health risk

Moderate (51-100)

Acceptable for most people, but sensitive individuals may experience minor issues

Unhealthy (101-200)

Everyone may experience health effects; sensitive groups more seriously affected

Hazardous (201+)

Emergency conditions affecting the entire population
